About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemical Engineering at Shiv Nadar University, Delhi NCR


Research

My interest lies in understanding the physics of soft matter systems using computational methods. Currently, I am working on computational modeling of Responsive Materials, Solvation Thermodynamics, Interfacial Phenomena, Statistical Mechanics and Liquid-liquid phase separation in synthetic and biological polymer solutions.
